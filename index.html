About

A storyteller raised on deadlines, sport, and a love of the long form.

I started in newsrooms — Times of India, Divya Bhaskar, MX Player and PressPlay — where I learned that the best stories never sit on the surface.

Today I host Level Up with Anona, a long-form podcast that invites doctors, founders, athletes and artists into honest, unrushed conversation.

A national-level table tennis player and now Growth & Strategy Head at VAANI Studio, I sit at the intersection of media, performance and the creator economy.
